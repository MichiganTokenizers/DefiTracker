Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026 | Version 1.0

Michigan Tokenizers LLC ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates the YieldLife service ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Data Type Purpose Required
Email Address Account creation, verification, password recovery, newsletter (if subscribed) Required for email auth; Optional for wallet auth
Password Account authentication Required for email auth only
Newsletter Preference Email communication preferences Optional

1.2 Information Collected Through Wallet Connection

Data Type Purpose
Cardano Wallet Address Account authentication, portfolio tracking, position queries
Wallet Type Identifying which wallet software you use (Nami, Eternl, Lace, etc.) for compatibility

Important: We never request, store, or have access to your wallet's private keys, seed phrases, or spending credentials. Wallet authentication uses cryptographic signatures that prove ownership without revealing sensitive information.

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Purpose
DeFi Position Data Entry dates, transaction hashes, price ratios for your tracked liquidity pool and lending positions
Saved Chart Configurations Your custom chart settings and preferences
Session Data Authentication state via secure cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Authenticate your identity, display your DeFi positions, and save your preferences
  • Send Transactional Communications: Email verification, password reset, and account notifications
  • Send Marketing Communications: Newsletter updates (only if you have opted in; you may unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

3. Information Sharing with Third Parties

We share your information with the following categories of third parties:

3.1 Blockchain Data Providers

To fetch your on-chain position data, we send your wallet address to:

  • Blockfrost API: Cardano blockchain data provider for querying UTXOs, transaction history, and token balances

3.2 DeFi Protocol APIs

To retrieve yield and position data, we query the following protocol APIs with your wallet address:

  • Minswap: Liquidity pool data
  • SundaeSwap: Liquidity pool and yield data
  • WingRiders: Liquidity pool data
  • Liqwid Finance: Lending market data

3.3 Email Service Providers

We use third-party email services to send transactional and marketing emails. Currently, emails are sent via Gmail SMTP servers.

3.4 No Third-Party Analytics

We do not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not sell your personal information to any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt; we never store plaintext passwords
  • Secure Sessions: Authentication uses HTTP-only, secure cookies with SameSite protection
  • Encrypted Transport: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database Security: User data is stored in PostgreSQL databases with access controls

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained until you request account deletion
  • Position History: Retained to provide historical performance analysis
  • Authentication Tokens: Temporary tokens (email verification, password reset) expire automatically

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View your personal data through your account settings
  • Correction: Update your email address at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Newsletter Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any newsletter
  • Disconnect Wallet: Log out to disconnect your wallet; you can create a new account at any time

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Essential for maintaining your logged-in state; these are HTTP-only and expire when you close your browser or after a period of inactivity

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from your country. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page
  • Sending email notification for material changes (if you have provided an email)
  • Requiring re-acceptance of Terms for significant changes

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt-out of sales (though we do not sell personal information).
